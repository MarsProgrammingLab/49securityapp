About 49th Security Division


  • Since 2009 the 49th Security Division (49SD) has stood as the flagship organization for the College of Computing and Informatics.

  • 49SD has made appearances on every local news network in Charlotte, has placed highly in regional, national, and international competitions and projects bringing fame and notoriety to UNC Charlotte and the department alike.

  • 49SD has direct contacts with well-known entities such as the NSA, Department of Defense, Bank of America, Microsoft, Google, Wells-Fargo, TIA-CREF, and many more agencies/corporations.
